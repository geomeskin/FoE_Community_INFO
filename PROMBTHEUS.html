Fragment Tracker — balance = received − assembled × required. Frags/Day = historical fragment accumulation rate from GEx, quests & events (excludes shop purchases). Divide Need by this to estimate days remaining. Est. Days and Est. Date are based on that rate. In-progress items shown first.
⚠️ Known data limitations: Fragments purchased in the QI Shop are not recorded in the FoE Helper export and will not appear here. Likewise, QI Seasonal Kit fragment balances (e.g. QI Spring/Summer/Autumn Epic Selection Kits) are not captured. If your in-game inventory shows fragment counts that don’t match this tracker, QI shop purchases are the likely cause.
