🏛 GB Planner

Tab 3 — Great Building levels, progress, and FP cost to next level for each player

What it does

Shows every tracked player's Great Buildings — current level, progress toward max, and how many Forge Points it costs to reach the next level. Useful for coordinating FP swaps and knowing whose GBs need contributions.

What you'll see

  • One section per player (one section = one MegaExport zip).
  • Player name, era, and account tag shown in the section header.
  • Each row is one Great Building.
  • Progress bar shows current level as a percentage of the GB's maximum level.
  • FP shown is the cost to reach the next level from where the player currently is.
  • GBs already at max level appear at the bottom in a muted style.
  • Filter pills at the top let you jump to a specific GB across all players at once.

Column guide

ColumnMeaning
Great BuildingName of the GB
LevelCurrent level / maximum level
ProgressVisual bar — how far to max
FP to NextForge Points needed to level up once

Data source & updates

Pulled from the greatBuildings section of each player's FoE Helper MegaExport zip in the exports/ folder.

Updated every Thursday when players run ./update.sh. To add a new player, drop their MegaExport zip (renamed to PLAYERTAG_foe_helper_...zip) into exports/ and run the script.

FP values are point-in-time. If a player leveled a GB between exports, the FP shown here may be stale until the next update run.
